Lab 4 - Pseudocode

Challenge

The purpose of the lab was to show that writing pseudocode can help you problem solve through your coding problems.

Problems

I did not feel very good on the day I was working on this project. My abdomen was in a lot of pain and I had to take a lot of breaks to cope with the cramping. But other than that, I did not really encounter any problems with the assignment itself.

Reflection

This assignment reminds me of my other coding assignments I had in community college where we had to draw out our code in order to understand what we were doing. I still draw out my code to this day, and it was one of the most helpful skills I learned for coding. Pseudocode is a very similar process to this, and I recognize the value in writing out good pseudocode.

Results

Pseudocode for putting on makeup!

  1. Prep face with moisturizer and sunscreen, rub the product onto the whole face gently.
  2. Take out eyebrow products, eyeshadow and brush, and fill in brows.
  3. Take out eyeshadow and eyeshadow brush, and brush the product onto eyelids.
  4. Put on eyeliner with eyeliner felt tip brush.
  5. Take out the lipstick product, and fill in the top and bottom lip with the chosen shade.
  6. Take out a big brush, and the blush product. Brush blush on face.
